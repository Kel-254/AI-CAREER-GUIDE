Connect with Experienced Mentors

Mentorship helps you avoid common mistakes and accelerates growth. Here’s how to get started:

  • Join online mentorship platforms or incubators.
  • Reach out to experienced entrepreneurs in your network.
  • Attend startup workshops and networking events.
  • Participate in startup competitions to get mentor feedback.
