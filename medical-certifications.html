Certifications to Boost Your Career

  • Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA): Essential for clinical and patient-care roles.
  • Certified Medical Assistant (CMA): Recognized for administrative and clinical medical skills.
  • Basic Life Support (BLS) & Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support (ACLS): Required for emergency preparedness.
  • Specialty Certifications: Oncology, Pediatrics, Radiology, etc., for career specialization.
  • Online Medical Courses: Platforms like Coursera, edX, and MedlinePlus for skill-building.
