Step-by-Step Startup Guide

Starting a business can feel overwhelming. This guide walks you through the key steps:

  • Ideation: Identify problems and opportunities worth solving.
  • Market Research: Analyze competitors and validate your idea.
  • Business Plan: Define vision, mission, goals, and strategy.
  • Legal Setup: Register your business and obtain necessary licenses.
  • Funding: Explore grants, investors, loans, and crowdfunding.
  • Launch: Develop MVP, test, and start reaching customers.
  • Growth: Scale operations, improve products, and expand markets.
